Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2025

Abroad Jobs ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Abroad Jobs mobile application and website located at https://emps.co.in (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register for our Service, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password
  • Profile Information: Professional experience, education, skills, resume/CV
  • Contact Information: Postal address, location preferences
  • Identity Verification: Date of birth, government-issued ID documents (for verification purposes)
  • Job Preferences: Desired job categories, salary expectations, location preferences

Contact Information Access

Important: Our mobile application requests access to your device's contact list for the following specific purposes:

  • Recruiter Network Building: Recruiters can sync their professional contacts to build their network within the platform
  • Job Referrals: To facilitate job referrals and professional networking between users
  • Contact Matching: To help you find colleagues and professional connections who are also using our platform

Contact Data Protection:

  • Contact information is encrypted and stored securely on our servers
  • We only access contacts when you explicitly grant permission
  • Contact data is used solely for professional networking and job-related purposes
  • We do not share your contacts with third parties without your consent
  • You can revoke contact access at any time through your device settings

Photo Selection (No Permissions Required)

Modern Privacy-Friendly Approach: Our mobile application uses the Android Photo Picker for image selection, which provides the following benefits:

  • No Permissions Required: We don't request access to your photo library
  • User Control: You explicitly choose which photos to share with our app
  • Privacy Protection: We only access the specific images you select
  • Secure Selection: The system handles photo access, not our app

Photo Selection Uses:

  • Profile Pictures: Upload profile photos for your job seeker or recruiter profile
  • Job Posting Images: Recruiters can add images to job postings to make them more attractive
  • Document Images: Upload photos of certificates or other job-related documents

The Photo Picker ensures your privacy by only allowing access to images you explicitly select, without requiring broad access to your photo library.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our job portal Service
  • To match job seekers with relevant job opportunities
  • To enable recruiters to find suitable candidates
  • To facilitate professional networking and referrals
  • To send you job alerts and notifications
  • To improve our Service and user experience
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Employers: When you apply for jobs, your profile information is shared with potential employers
  • With Recruiters: Your profile may be visible to recruiters for job matching purposes

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Specifically:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 2 years after account deletion
  • Contact Information: Retained while you have contact sync enabled and deleted when you disable the feature
  • Job Application Data: Retained for 3 years for employment verification purposes
  • Log Data: Retained for 12 months for service improvement and security purposes

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data we hold
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Contact Sync Control: Enable or disable contact synchronization in app settings

Permissions Explained

Our mobile app requests the following permissions:

  • Contacts: To sync your professional network and enable job referrals (optional)
  • Internet: To connect to our servers and provide job portal services (required)
  • Notifications: To send job alerts and important updates (optional)
  • Location: To show location-based job opportunities (optional)

No Photo/Media Permissions Required: We use the Android Photo Picker for image selection, which doesn't require any permissions and provides better privacy protection.

You can manage these permissions through your device settings at any time.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Employee training on data protection

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

International Data Transfers

As we provide international job placement services, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove such information.

Third-Party Services

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. For significant changes, we may also send you an email notification.

Google Play Policy Compliance

Our app complies with Google Play's privacy and data handling requirements:

  • Contact Permission: Used exclusively for professional networking and job referral features within our job portal platform
  • Photo Picker API: We use the modern Android Photo Picker instead of requesting media permissions, providing better privacy protection
  • Data Minimization: We only collect data necessary for our core job portal functionality
  • User Control: All sensitive permissions are optional and can be managed through device settings
  • Transparency: This privacy policy clearly explains all data collection and usage practices
  • No Persistent Media Access: We don't store or access your photo library - only images you explicitly select

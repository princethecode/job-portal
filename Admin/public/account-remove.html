Account Removal Request

We're sorry to see you go. Please read the information below before proceeding.

⚠️ Important Notice

• Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone

• All your data including profile, applications, and saved jobs will be permanently deleted

• You will lose access to all job applications and their status

• This action may take up to 30 days to complete

What happens when you delete your account?

  • Your profile and personal information will be permanently removed
  • All job applications submitted through our platform will be deleted
  • Your resume and uploaded documents will be permanently deleted
  • You will be unsubscribed from all notifications and emails
  • Your account cannot be recovered after deletion

Before you delete your account

Consider these alternatives:

  • Deactivate temporarily: You can deactivate your account instead of deleting it
  • Update preferences: Adjust your notification and privacy settings
  • Contact support: We're here to help resolve any issues you might have
